About ELMINIC Breadhaus

Our Bakery Starts when my grandmother Feliza Dela Rosa learned how to bake. She told us that she will try to open up a little bakery. Our breads in the very first day of opening was sold out. So my grandma decided to continue the business until now. Our breads are delicious so Im inviting you to try it especially our banana and carrot cakes.

ELMINIC Breadhaus is a little bakery and has a goal to become a restaurant in the near future, but only that we are a small bakery, many customers still enjoy and keep on ordering some of our breads so the goal is not a dream but is yet to come.

There are 5 members in the little bakery that keeps it working,(1) The ELMINIC(Ellie,Mikko,Dominic) or the siblings are the one who sell the breads to the customers.(2) The Baker(Feliza Dela Rosa) the grandmother of the siblings is the one who bakes the breads based from her experience in Hong Kong and last but not the least.(3)The right Hand(Salvador) The husband of the baker who helps in heavy baking that can't be done just by a single person.
